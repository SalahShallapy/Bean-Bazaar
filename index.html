Our History

Beaz Bazaar began as a dream to create a space where coffee lovers could come together and enjoy the simple pleasures of life. Founded in 2015, our journey started with a small team of passionate baristas and a vision to bring the finest coffee to Cairo. Over the years, we’ve grown from a humble corner café to a beloved community hub, all while staying true to our roots. We pride ourselves on sourcing the best beans from around the world and crafting each cup with love and dedication .
